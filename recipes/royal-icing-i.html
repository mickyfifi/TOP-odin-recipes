Royal Icing I

Description: Perfect icing for your gingerbread houses!

By Diane

Ingredients

  • 4 egg whites
  • 4 cups sifted confectioners' sugar
  • 1 teaspoon lemon extract

Directions

  1. Beat egg whites in a clean, large bowl with mixer at high speed until foamy (use only grade A clean, uncracked eggs). Gradually add sugar and lemon extract. Beat at high speed until thickened.

Cook's Note:

When dry, royal icing is very hard and resistant to damage that can occur during shipping/handling.
